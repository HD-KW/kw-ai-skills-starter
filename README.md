## SIMPLE SETUP — Recommended for Most Agents
## No GitHub account needed — takes about 5 minutes

---

### Part 1: Download the Starter Kit

Step 1: Look at the top of this page
        You will see a green button that says Code
        with a small arrow next to it

Step 2: Click that green Code button

Step 3: A small menu will appear with a few options
        Look for the option that says Download ZIP
        Click Download ZIP

Step 4: Your computer will download a file called
        kw-ai-skills-starter-main.zip
        It will appear in your Downloads folder

Step 5: Find that zip file in your Downloads folder
        Double click it to unzip it
        You will see a new folder appear called
        kw-ai-skills-starter-main

Step 6: Drag that folder to your Desktop
        so it is easy to find

---

### Part 2: Open the Starter Kit in Cursor

Step 1: Go back to Cursor on your computer

Step 2: Click File at the top menu
        Then click Open Folder

Step 3: Navigate to your Desktop
        Click on kw-ai-skills-starter-main
        Click Open

Step 4: You should now see a list of folders 
        on the left side of Cursor that looks like this:

        agents
            skills
            marketing
            neighborhood-profiles
            templates
            archive
        .cursorrules
        README.md

        If you can see that list you are in the right place.

---

### Part 3: Fill Out Your Context File

This is the most important step.
The AI is only as good as the information you give it.
Budget 45 to 60 minutes for this step.

Step 1: In the left panel of Cursor
        Click the agents folder to open it
        Click the skills folder to open it
        Click on real-estate-context-TEMPLATE.md

Step 2: You will see a file full of instructions
        and sections marked with brackets like this:
        [YOUR NAME]
        [YOUR PHONE]
        [TARGET NEIGHBORHOOD]

Step 3: Replace every single bracket with your 
        real information
        Do not leave any brackets blank
        Every section you fill in makes your 
        AI assistant more powerful and specific

Step 4: When you have filled out every section
        click File at the top menu
        click Save As
        name the file: real-estate-context.md
        make sure you are saving it inside the
        agents/skills/ folder
        click Save

---

### Part 4: Test Your AI Assistant

Step 1: In Cursor press Command + L on Mac
        or Ctrl + L on Windows
        A chat panel will open on the right side

Step 2: Type this exactly and press Enter:

        Who are you and what files are you working from?

Step 3: Your AI assistant should respond and mention
        your skill files and Keller Williams
        If it does you are live and ready to go

Step 4: Try your first real request:

        Draft a listing for 123 Main Street
        3 bedrooms 2 bathrooms 1800 square feet
        Updated kitchen new roof asking $450,000

        Watch how it responds — it will ask for any 
        missing details before writing anything

---

### You Are Live

From this point forward you can ask your AI assistant 
to help with any real estate task and it will follow 
your specific playbooks instead of giving generic advice.

## FULL SETUP — For Agents Who Want Updates Automatically
## Requires a free GitHub account

If you want to receive updates to the skill files 
automatically as they are improved follow these steps
instead of the Simple Setup above.

Step 1: Create a free GitHub account at github.com
Step 2: Come back to this page
Step 3: Click the green Use This Template button 
        at the top of this page
Step 4: Name your repository: real-estate-ai-skills
Step 5: Set it to Private
Step 6: Click Create Repository
Step 7: Download GitHub Desktop at desktop.github.com
Step 8: Sign in with your GitHub account
Step 9: Click File then Clone Repository
Step 10: Select real-estate-ai-skills
Step 11: Set the location to your Desktop
Step 12: Click Clone
Step 13: Open Cursor and follow Part 2 and 
         Part 3 from the Simple Setup above
         starting from Open the Starter Kit in Cursor

## Quick Command Reference

Once you are set up use these prompts to get started

Write a listing:
"Draft a listing for [ADDRESS]
[BEDS] bed [BATHS] bath [SQFT] square feet
[TOP FEATURES] asking $[PRICE]"

Handle an objection:
"How do I respond when a buyer says 
interest rates are too high"

Write social media content:
"Write me a full week of Instagram posts 
for my farm area in [NEIGHBORHOOD]"

Build a nurture sequence:
"Write a 5 email follow up sequence 
for an open house lead"

Write a video script:
"Write a 60 second Reel script about 
why buyers should not wait for rates to drop"

Build a farming campaign:
"Write a just sold postcard for [ADDRESS] 
targeting my farm area"

Check for compliance:
"Check this listing copy for fair housing compliance:
[PASTE YOUR COPY]"
