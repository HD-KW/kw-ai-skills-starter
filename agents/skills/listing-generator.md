## THE 5-LAYER LISTING FORMULA

### Layer 1: The Hook (First 2 sentences)
Lead with LIFESTYLE not square footage
Answer: "What does it FEEL like to live here?"
Use present tense

DO THIS:
"Sunday mornings in [NEIGHBORHOOD] move at their own pace — 
farmers market by 9, coffee on your wraparound porch by 10. 
This 4-bedroom craftsman was built for exactly that kind of life."

NOT THIS:
"Welcome to this beautiful 4-bedroom home!"

---

### Layer 2: The Property Story (2-3 sentences)
Translate features into benefits
Formula: "[Feature] means [specific life benefit]"

DO THIS:
"The open-concept kitchen — anchored by a 10-foot island and 
pro-grade range — means you're never separated from the conversation."

NOT THIS:
"Large kitchen with island and stainless appliances."

---

### Layer 3: The Upgrade Confidence Builder (1-2 sentences)
Mention recent updates to reduce buyer anxiety
Frame as "done for you" value

DO THIS:
"With a new roof (2022), HVAC (2023), and updated electrical, 
the big-ticket items are handled — move in and enjoy."

---

### Layer 4: The Neighborhood Anchor (2-3 sentences)
Pull lifestyle details from real-estate-context.md
Include one specific verifiable local detail

DO THIS:
"[SCHOOL NAME] is a 6-minute walk. The weekend farmers market 
is 4 blocks east. And [COFFEE SHOP] already knows your order."

---

### Layer 5: The Call to Action (1 sentence)
Create urgency without pressure

DO THIS:
"Homes like this in [NEIGHBORHOOD] averaged 9 days on market 
last quarter — schedule your private showing before the weekend."

NOT THIS:
"Won't last long! Call today!!!"
