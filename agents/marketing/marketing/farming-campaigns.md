## THE FARMING PHILOSOPHY
Farming is not about blasting your name everywhere.
It is about becoming so genuinely useful to a neighborhood 
that when anyone on those streets thinks about real estate 
they think of you first and only.

The agents who win in farming are not the loudest.
They are the most consistent and the most relevant.
Show up with value every single time and the listings follow.

## THE FARMING MATH
Before choosing or expanding a farm area run this calculation

Total homes in target farm area: _____
Your goal market share percentage: _____%
Homes you need to sell to hit goal: _____

Minimum recommended farm size: 400 to 500 homes
Maximum recommended farm size for one agent: 800 to 1000 homes
Minimum touches per year to build recognition: 12
Minimum touches per year to build dominance: 18 to 24

### Just Listed Door Knocking Script

Knock on the 20 homes closest to the new listing.
These are your highest value conversations.

Opening at the door:
"Hi there — I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams.
I am not here to bother you — I just wanted to let you 
know personally that [ADDRESS] just hit the market 
a few doors down and I thought you might want to know 
before the sign goes up."

If they engage:
"It is listed at $[PRICE] — [BEDS] beds and [BATHS] baths.
The reason I stop by in person is because a new listing 
in your neighborhood is actually relevant to your home value.
When comparable homes sell it updates the data that 
determines what your home is worth.

Do you have any sense of what your home might be worth 
in today's market?"

If they say no or they are curious:
"I would be happy to put together a quick snapshot for you — 
completely free, no obligation. It takes me about 10 minutes 
and I can email it to you or drop it by.
What would be easier for you?"

If they say they are thinking of selling:
"That is great timing actually. With [ADDRESS] hitting the 
market right now there is real buyer activity in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Would it be worth 20 minutes to sit down and look at 
what your home could realistically sell for right now?"

Leave behind:
A just listed flyer with your contact information
A handwritten note if they were not home

## CAMPAIGN 4: ABSENTEE OWNER CAMPAIGN

Absentee owners are landlords who live outside the area.
They are often ready to sell but nobody is talking to them.
This is one of the most underused farming strategies in KW.

### How to Find Absentee Owners
Pull from your county assessor records
Look for properties where the mailing address is different 
from the property address
These are your absentee owner targets

### Absentee Owner Letter — First Touch

"Dear [NEIGHBORHOOD] Property Owner,

My name is [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams [MARKET CENTER].
I specialize in [NEIGHBORHOOD] and I am writing to share 
something you may find useful as a property owner in this area.

The [NEIGHBORHOOD] market has changed significantly.
Here is a quick snapshot of where things stand right now:

Median sale price: $[X] — up [X]% from [TIMEFRAME]
Average days on market: [X] days
Current months of inventory: [X]

If you purchased your property before [YEAR], you are 
likely sitting on significant equity that many owners 
in your position are not fully aware of.

I am not assuming you want to sell — many of my clients 
simply want to understand their position before making 
any decisions. That is completely reasonable.

If you would like a free, no-obligation current market 
analysis of your [NEIGHBORHOOD] property I would be 
happy to provide one. It takes about 10 minutes and 
gives you a clear picture of where you stand today.

You can reach me at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
I look forward to being a useful resource for you.

## CAMPAIGN 5: EXPIRED LISTING CAMPAIGN

Expired listings are sellers who want to sell but had 
a bad experience. They need a reason to trust again.
Approach with empathy first and strategy second.

### Expired Listing Letter — First Touch

"Dear [HOMEOWNER NAME],

Your home at [ADDRESS] recently came off the market, 
and I want to reach out — not to pitch you, but because 
I have specific insight into why homes in [NEIGHBORHOOD] 
sometimes do not sell, and it is not always what people think.

In most cases it comes down to one of three things:
Pricing strategy that did not match current buyer behavior
Marketing that reached the wrong audience
Timing that worked against the property

I have sold [X] homes in [NEIGHBORHOOD] in the last 
[TIMEFRAME] and I have a clear picture of what buyers 
in this specific market are responding to right now.

I am not going to promise you a number that sounds good 
but does not reflect reality. What I will promise is 
an honest assessment of what went wrong and a specific 
plan for what to do differently.

If a straight-forward conversation about your options 
would be useful, I am available at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
No pressure. No pitch. Just a real conversation.

[YOUR NAME]
Keller Williams [MARKET CENTER]
License [NUMBER]

Equal Housing Opportunity"

## DOOR KNOCKING MASTER SCRIPT

Use this as your base script for any door knocking situation.
Adapt the opening based on the specific campaign.

The Opening — first 10 seconds matter most:
"Hi there — I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams.
I am not here to take up your time — 
I just wanted to [SPECIFIC REASON tied to campaign]."

The Value Bridge:
Always connect your reason for knocking to their home value
"The reason this matters to you is [CONNECTION TO THEIR EQUITY 
OR HOME VALUE OR NEIGHBORHOOD]"

The Qualifying Question:
"Have you given any thought to [BUYING OR SELLING OR 
WHAT YOUR HOME IS WORTH] in the next [TIMEFRAME]?"

The Offer:
"I would be happy to [SPECIFIC VALUE OFFER — free CMA, 
market report, buyer introduction] — 
no cost and no obligation."

The Leave Behind:
Always leave something physical with your contact information
A just sold or just listed flyer
A market report
A business card with a handwritten note

If They Are Not Home:
Leave a handwritten note — never just a flyer alone
"Hi [NEIGHBOR] — I stopped by to share some exciting 
neighborhood news. Give me a call when you have a moment.
[YOUR NAME] — [PHONE]"

## FARMING CAMPAIGN COMPLIANCE RULES

All farming materials must pass fair-housing-compliance.md

Never use neighborhood descriptions that imply 
demographic preferences in any mailer or script

Never target or exclude any farm area based on 
the racial or ethnic composition of the neighborhood

All statistical claims must be sourced and dated

All mailers must include Equal Housing Opportunity statement

Door knocking rules:
Respect No Soliciting signs — do not knock on these doors
Never knock before 9am or after 7pm
Never knock on Sundays in conservative markets
Always have your license number available if asked
