# Skill: Lead Nurture
# Keller Williams | Email and Text Sequence Playbook

---

## WHAT THIS FILE DOES
Gives the AI specific email and text sequences for every 
lead type a KW agent encounters so follow-up is always 
timely, personal, and value-driven rather than generic 
and forgettable.

---

## THE GOLDEN RULE OF NURTURE
Every single touchpoint must pass this test before sending:
"Would I want to receive this if I were the lead?"

Give value first
Make one ask per message — never two
Never send two check-ins in a row without new information
Personalize beyond first name — reference their specific situation
Pause all sequences the moment a lead responds — human takes over

---

## LEAD TYPE QUICK REFERENCE

Hot Lead
Definition: Pre-approved, actively searching, ready in 0 to 30 days
Goal: Showing appointment within 72 hours

Warm Lead
Definition: Researching, no pre-approval yet, 30 to 90 days out
Goal: Become their trusted source before they are ready to move

Cold Lead
Definition: Just looking, 6 or more months out, or timeline unclear
Goal: Stay top of mind without being annoying

Seller Lead
Definition: Thinking of listing but not yet committed
Goal: Earn the listing appointment

Past Client
Definition: Previously closed — maintaining the relationship
Goal: Referrals and repeat business

Ghost Lead
Definition: Went dark after initial contact
Goal: Re-engage without burning the bridge

## UNIVERSAL COMMUNICATION RULES

Text Message Standards:
Keep opening line under 160 characters
Ask one question or make one ask — never both in the same message
Never text before 8am or after 8pm local time
Always identify yourself — never assume they saved your number
Respond to any reply within 5 minutes during business hours

Email Standards:
Subject lines should be 6 to 10 words — specific beats clever
Write preview text of 10 words or less that creates curiosity
Include one call to action per email — never give two options
Use short paragraphs with white space — most people read on mobile
Include an unsubscribe option on every email for CAN-SPAM compliance

KW Command CRM Notes:
Log every single touchpoint in Command immediately
Tag the lead type on first contact
Assign a SmartPlan based on lead temperature
Review sequence open rates and reply rates monthly
Pause the SmartPlan the moment a lead responds

The Non-Negotiable Rule:
If you would not be comfortable receiving this message 
from a service provider yourself — do not send it.
