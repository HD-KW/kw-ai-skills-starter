## WHY VIDEO MATTERS FOR KW AGENTS
Video is the fastest way to build trust at scale.
A prospect who watches 3 of your videos feels like 
they already know you before they ever reach out.
That is the most powerful lead warming tool available.
You do not need a production team or a ring light.
You need a clear message and a repeatable framework.

---

## THE VIDEO CONTENT HIERARCHY

Level 1: Short Form Video — 15 to 90 seconds
Platforms: Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
Purpose: Reach new audiences and build awareness
Frequency: 3 to 5 times per week
Skill required: Low — just a phone and good lighting

Level 2: Medium Form Video — 2 to 5 minutes
Platforms: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube
Purpose: Educate your existing audience and build authority
Frequency: 1 to 2 times per week
Skill required: Medium — basic editing helps

Level 3: Long Form Video — 8 to 20 minutes
Platforms: YouTube primarily
Purpose: Deep authority building and search visibility
Frequency: 1 to 2 times per month
Skill required: Medium to high — scripting and editing needed

## SHORT FORM VIDEO SCRIPTS

### FRAMEWORK 1: The Myth Buster — 30 to 60 seconds

This format gets high shares because it challenges 
something people already believe.

Hook — first 3 seconds — say this directly to camera:
"Everyone says [COMMON REAL ESTATE MYTH] — 
and it is costing [BUYERS OR SELLERS] money."

Body — 3 punchy points:
"Here is the truth. [POINT 1 in one sentence.]
[POINT 2 in one sentence.]
[POINT 3 in one sentence.]"

Close:
"Follow me for real [CITY] real estate truth 
that most agents will not tell you."

Example script:
"Everyone says you should wait for interest rates to drop 
before buying — and it is costing buyers thousands.
Here is the truth.
When rates drop prices go up because every waiting buyer 
floods the market at once.
The buyers who bought last year are already building equity 
while the waiters are still waiting.
Buy the home. Refinance the rate.
Follow me for real [CITY] market insight every week."

### FRAMEWORK 2: The Local Expert — 30 to 60 seconds

This format builds your neighborhood authority fast.
Only someone who actually knows the area can make this video.

Hook:
"I have sold [X] homes in [NEIGHBORHOOD] — 
here are 3 things I know that Zillow will never tell you."

Body:
"Number one. [INSIDER DETAIL about the neighborhood]
Number two. [INSIDER DETAIL about the neighborhood]
Number three. [INSIDER DETAIL about the neighborhood]"

Close:
"Want the full picture on [NEIGHBORHOOD]?
Send me a DM and I will walk you through everything."

Example insider details:
"The homes on the north side of [STREET] back to a greenbelt 
and sell for an average of $18,000 more than identical homes 
on the south side."
"[NEIGHBORHOOD] has been averaging 8 days on market — 
if you are not pre-approved you are already behind."
"The new [DEVELOPMENT] opening in Q1 is going to push 
values on the east side of [NEIGHBORHOOD] significantly."

### FRAMEWORK 3: The Process Explainer — 45 to 90 seconds

First time buyers and sellers share these constantly.
This is your highest reach content type.

Hook:
"Nobody explains [CONFUSING PART OF THE PROCESS] 
so I am going to do it right now in under 60 seconds."

Body — walk through 3 to 5 simple steps:
"Step one. [SIMPLE EXPLANATION]
Step two. [SIMPLE EXPLANATION]
Step three. [SIMPLE EXPLANATION]"

Close:
"Save this video for when you are ready to [BUY OR SELL].
And follow me — I explain the whole process 
one video at a time."

Process topics that perform best:
How offers actually work
What happens at a home inspection
What closing costs are and who pays them
How escrow works in plain language
What a pre-approval actually means
The difference between list price and sale price
What contingencies protect you from
How to read a sellers disclosure

### FRAMEWORK 4: The Market Update — 45 to 60 seconds

Post this every month for each farm neighborhood.
This is your single most important authority-building video.

Hook:
"Here is exactly what happened in the [NEIGHBORHOOD] 
real estate market in [MONTH] — no fluff just data."

Body:
"[X] homes sold last month.
Median sale price: $[X] — that is [up or down] [X]% 
from this time last year.
Average days on market: [X] days.
Homes selling above asking price: [X]%.

What does this mean for you?
[1 to 2 sentences in plain language about what buyers 
or sellers should know right now]"

Close:
"I post this every single month for [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Follow so you never miss an update."

### FRAMEWORK 5: The Hot Take — 30 to 45 seconds

Opinions get more engagement than facts alone.

Hook:

HAPPENING IN YOUR MARKET]"

Body:
"Here is why I think this.
[POINT 1]
[POINT 2]
[EVIDENCE OR DATA that supports your take]"

Close:
"Agree or disagree? Tell me in the comments.
And follow for more honest [CITY] real estate takes."

## MEDIUM FORM VIDEO SCRIPTS

### FRAMEWORK 6: The Neighborhood Tour — 3 to 5 minutes

Film this while walking or driving through your farm area.
This is the highest trust video format for local authority.

Opening — while standing in the neighborhood:
"I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams [MARKET CENTER] 
and today I am taking you inside [NEIGHBORHOOD] — 
one of the most [CHARACTERISTIC] areas in [CITY].
I have sold [X] homes here and I am going to show you 
exactly what makes this neighborhood special 
and what the market is doing right now."

Section 1: The Vibe — 45 to 60 seconds
Walk through the main street or park while talking
"The first thing people notice about [NEIGHBORHOOD] is [OBSERVATION].
The people who love it here tend to [LIFESTYLE DESCRIPTION].
On a typical [DAY OF WEEK] you will see [SPECIFIC SCENE]."

Section 2: The Lifestyle Anchors — 60 to 90 seconds
Point out 3 to 5 specific local spots
"Right here is [BUSINESS NAME] — [why locals love it].
Two blocks that way is [PARK OR FEATURE] — [what makes it special].
And [SCHOOL NAME] is right around the corner — 
rated [X] out of 10 on GreatSchools."

Section 3: The Market Data — 60 seconds
Face the camera and share the numbers
"Here is what the market is doing in [NEIGHBORHOOD] right now.
Median sale price is $[X].
Homes are averaging [X] days on market.
[X]% of homes are selling above asking price.
[1 sentence about what this means for buyers or sellers]"

Section 4: The Trade-Off — 30 seconds
Be honest — this builds massive trust
"Now here is the one thing I always tell people upfront 
about [NEIGHBORHOOD]: [HONEST TRADE-OFF OR LIMITATION].
For some people that is a deal-breaker. For others it is not.
I would rather you know now."

Close:
"If you want to see what is available in [NEIGHBORHOOD] 
right now or get a full market report sent to you, 
DM me the word [NEIGHBORHOOD] and I will send it over.
I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams [MARKET CENTER] — 
see you in the next one."

### FRAMEWORK 7: The Buyer or Seller Guide — 3 to 5 minutes

Use this to walk through a complete process step by step.
This positions you as the most helpful agent in your market.

Opening:
"If you are thinking about [BUYING OR SELLING] in [CITY] 
in the next [TIMEFRAME] this video is going to save you 
from the 3 most expensive mistakes I see people make.
I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams and I have helped 
[X] people [BUY OR SELL] in this market.
Let me walk you through exactly what you need to know."

For each mistake or step — use this structure:
"[MISTAKE OR STEP NUMBER]: [NAME IT]
Here is what most people do: [COMMON WRONG APPROACH]
Here is what you should do instead: [YOUR RECOMMENDATION]
Here is why it matters: [CONSEQUENCE OF GETTING IT WRONG]"

Close:
"If you found this helpful save it and share it with 
someone who is thinking about [BUYING OR SELLING].
And if you are ready to start the process reach out directly — 
my contact is in the description.
I am [YOUR NAME] — see you next week."

## LONG FORM VIDEO SCRIPTS

### FRAMEWORK 8: The Complete Neighborhood Guide — 8 to 12 minutes

This is your highest authority video format.
It ranks on YouTube and drives organic leads for years.

Title formula:
"Living in [NEIGHBORHOOD] [CITY] — Everything You Need to Know in [YEAR]"

Opening — 60 seconds:
"If you are considering moving to [NEIGHBORHOOD] in [CITY] 
this is the most complete and honest guide you will find.
I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams [MARKET CENTER] 
and I have personally sold [X] homes in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
I am going to cover the lifestyle, the schools, the commute, 
the market data, and the one thing most people do not find out 
until after they move in.
Let us get into it."

Chapter 1: The Neighborhood Overview — 90 seconds
History, character, vibe, who lives there, what has changed

Chapter 2: The Lifestyle — 2 minutes
Restaurants, coffee, parks, walkability, weekend activities
Be specific — name real places with real details

Chapter 3: The Schools — 60 seconds
Name each school, cite GreatSchools ratings with dates
Mention notable programs if relevant
Note: Never steer based on school demographics

Chapter 4: The Commute — 60 seconds
Distance to major employment centers
Highway access, public transit options
Be honest about traffic — do not oversell

Chapter 5: The Market Data — 90 seconds
Current median price, days on market, list-to-sale ratio
Year over year trend
What buyers and sellers should know right now

Chapter 6: The Honest Trade-Off — 60 seconds
What is the one downside most people do not discover 
until after they move in
This section builds more trust than any other part of the video

Chapter 7: The Call to Action — 30 seconds
"If you are thinking about buying or selling in [NEIGHBORHOOD] 
I would love to help. Reach out directly at [CONTACT INFO].
Subscribe for monthly market updates on [NEIGHBORHOOD] 
and every other major area in [CITY].
I am [YOUR NAME] with Keller Williams — see you in the next one."

## VIDEO PRODUCTION RULES

Before You Film:
Know your hook before you press record
Have your 3 main points ready — do not wing it
Check your background — clean and neutral is best
Check your lighting — face a window for natural light
Check your audio — quiet room, phone close to your face

While You Film:
Look at the camera lens not the screen
Speak slightly faster than feels comfortable — 
nervous speakers slow down too much
Use hand gestures — they make you look more confident
If you mess up keep going — edit it out later
Film multiple takes of your hook — it is the most important part

After You Film:
Caption every video — most people watch without sound
Keep captions accurate — auto-generated captions have errors
Add your name and brokerage as a text overlay
Add your contact information at the end
Review for any fair housing language before posting
